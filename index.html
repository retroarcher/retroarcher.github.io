RetroArcher

Retro gaming in Plex!

What is RetroArcher?

RetroArcher is a gaming plug-in for Plex Media Server! The goal of RetroArcher is to use Plex as a gaming front end interface and then use proven game streaming technology to stream your gameplay to any of your standard Plex clients. Simply put RetroArcher is to video games, what Plex is to multimedia.

Features

Consoles - Play all your favorite retro consoles

Handhelds - Add your favorite handhelds too

Arcades - “No tokens required”

Computers - All the classics!

Engines - And more…

Coming Soon - We’ll add support for these soon

Controllers - Nearly any bluetooth controller will work
